Fall Detection - Your Safety is Our Priority

Welcome to Fall Detection – the leading website that provides advanced technology to detect falls. Our state-of-the-art image input technology accurately detects whether a person in the image is falling or not. With our cutting-edge fall detection technology and emergency calling service, we save lives and ensure peace of mind for our valued users.

Services we provide

Image Input

Submit your images to our website and get accurate fall detection analysis through our advanced AI-powered algorithms.

Fall Detection

Our advanced AI-powered algorithms detect falls with high accuracy, providing immediate alerts to ensure timely intervention.

Emergency Calling

Our emergency calling feature provides immediate access to emergency services, ensuring timely intervention during falls or other emergencies.

Video Upload

Upload video footage of falls to our website and receive detailed analysis and reports on fall detection and prevention.
